Background

I learned to play the piano (7 years of classical, 3 years of jazz), taught myself a little acoustic guitar over the past years, and practiced the horn for 2 years at school. I participated in several orchestras, big bands, and other musical groups, and since the age of 11, I have written songs. With my former band KODA I had over 50 shows, performed over 40 original songs live, and released 2 physical albums in seven years. Furthermore, I've always been interested in music production which has not only provided me with technical expertise, but also with a rich toolbox of microphones, converters, software, and other musical gear, all of which I still use nowadays to work on amateur to semi-professional recordings for myself or other artists/bands.
